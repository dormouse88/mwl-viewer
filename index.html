Influence Reduction System

Each copy of a card from this list reduces an Identity's influence limit by 1 when included in a deck.

An Identity's printed influence limit cannot be reduced below 1.

This reduction occurs even if the card matches the Identity's faction.

Sources: Article and PDF.

Universal Influence System

Each copy of a card on this list has universal influence equal to its level.

Universal Influence is separate from printed influence, not a replacement.

Universal Influence always counts against an identity's influence limit, even if the card matches the identity's faction.

Sources: Article and PDF. Also, further information.

Banned and Restricted System

You may include one restricted card (up to its maximum number of copies) in your deck.

You may not include banned cards (also sometimes called 'removed' cards) in your deck.

Sources: Article and PDF.

Ban System

You may not include banned cards in your deck.

Source: Article.
